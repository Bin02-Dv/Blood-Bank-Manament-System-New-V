About VitaFlow

VitaFlow is a comprehensive blood bank management platform designed to streamline the entire lifecycle of blood donation—from donor registration and scheduling to inventory tracking and emergency fulfillment. Our mission is to bridge the gap between donors and those in need, ensuring no life is lost due to blood shortages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Generally, donors must be 17–65 years old, weigh at least 50kg, and be in good health. Use our eligibility checker for details.

How often can I donate?

Whole blood every 56 days; platelets every 7 days (up to 24 times/year).

Is my data safe?

Yes, we use encryption and comply with health data protection standards.
